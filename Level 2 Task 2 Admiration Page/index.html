Biography

Usain Bolt - The Worlds Fastest Man Alive

Usain Bolt is considered by many people to be the greatest sprinter in world history. He is currently (2021) the fastest person (based on the 100m) who ever lived. He holds the world records for both the 100m and 200m. He also shares the 4x100m relay world record with Jamaican teammates Yohan Blake, Nesta Carter, and Michael Frater. His surname, or last name, is very fitting of the world's fastest man because the word "bolt" is often associated with lightning, which symbolizes speed.
